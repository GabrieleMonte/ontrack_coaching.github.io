About Us

Our Mission

“To provide everyone with an opportunity to pursue your dreams athletically and academically through preparation, counseling and coaching. ”

What

OnTrack is a resource for students looking to increase their odds of gaining admission to a college of their choice by sharpening their skills both academically or athletically, or both. We show you the tools and techniques used by Ivy league student athletes and their coaches to guide you to not only understand various academic topics or perform your best athletically, but also with basic student life skills that will come in handy during your career at college and in your life in general.

Through our online medium, we give you access to exceptional instruction to cater your needs no matter where you may be in the world or what skill level you may currently possess. We provide guidance in efforts to give everyone the ability to pursue the college of their dreams.

Why

If you dream to go to a highly ranked research university, your best chance is to do that as a student athlete. A study by the Office of Population Research at Princeton University showed that the edge given to athletes is roughly equivalent to an extra 200 points on the SAT . This corresponds to the single biggest leg up one can get in the elite-college admissions game in the U.S, more than minorities or legacies., as noted in a Bloomberg article from March 2019 .

Nevertheless, often athletes with dreams of attending a highly acclaimed university fall short because they were ill-prepared for the academic requirement needed for admissions, or they don’t meet the athletic standards to be a viable candidate. We are here to change that.

How

All our operations take place online via our Zoom channel. We offer both group and one to one sessions for all our services. If you are interested in starting your journey with us, fill out the Form at the bottom of the page and we'll get back to you within 24 hr.
