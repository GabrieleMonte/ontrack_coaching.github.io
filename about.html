About Us

A note from the Founder

As a college coach and recruiter at a prestigious university, I receive many inquiries from student-athletes seeking admission. Sometimes I will get a qualified candidate, but many times, maybe 8 out of 10, the applicant won’t make the cut. Either they don’t quite have the athletic ability or they don’t meet the academic standards, or worse, they have neither. Often, it is NOT because they don’t have the ability. They are just unprepared.

OnTrack coaching and educational services gives everyone a shot. The team of coaches and teachers I have assembled are Olympians, National Champions, Conference Champions, Ivy League graduates, master teachers, experienced consultants and outstanding tutors, with a combined experience of over 75 years. If you are looking to go to college and want to increase your chances through track and field and academics, or you just want to see how far you can go in the world of track and field, my team and I can help.

Our Mission

To provide EVERYONE with an opportunity to pursue your dreams athletically and academically through preparation, counseling and coaching.
