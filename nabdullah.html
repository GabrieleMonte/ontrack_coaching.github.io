Nia Abdullah

About Me

Hello! My name is Nia Abdullah. I am a sophomore at Rutgers University-New Brunswick, majoring in English and minoring in both Creative Writing and Theater. I am an outstanding college student and during high school, I was a member of the National Honor Society and Latin Honor Society.

I have had the pleasure of working with students for many years, and I currently work at Kumon Math and Reading Center as a Junior Kumon instructor. At Kumon, I assist students (typically ages 3-9) in developing strong math and english skills. My hobbies include reading, writing, and acting. I enjoy creating poetry, short stories and monologues. I even had my short story “It Starts with a Cough” featured on Rutgers Writers House Online Journal. I am an enthusiastic mentor and teacher, and I look forward to working with students and helping them explore the fascinating realm of english!
