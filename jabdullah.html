Jada Abdullah

About Me

Hello! My name is Jada Abdullah. I am a senior at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, New Jersey, majoring in Political Science and minoring in History. I’m an honor student and a member of the prestigious Eagleton Institute of Politics. I have a lot of experience tutoring and mentoring students at all levels. I am a Reading Manager at Kumon Math and Reading Center, a peer educator at college, a Co-Founder of a youth empowerment group in my district, and a Youth Liaison for the Somerset County Democratic Committee Black Caucus-Youth Development.

I enjoy listening to and playing music, spending time with family and friends, taking nature walks, learning new things and volunteerism. A fun fact about me is I used to play trumpet, trombone, and baritone horn. I’m currently teaching myself how to play guitar. As a passionate educator and a great student, I look forward to working with middle school students in Math and History and high school students in U.S History. Thank you for considering me!
