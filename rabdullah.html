Robert Abdullah

About Me

My name is Robert Abdullah and I have been coaching track and field for over 30 years, from private lessons to grade school, to college and beyond. I have coached conference champions, state champions, national champions, NCAA All-Americans and Olympians. I have been mentored by some of the greatest coaches ever: Wilbur Ross, Hall of Fame coach and author of the Hurdler’s Bible; Steve Riddick, Olympic Gold Medalist and world ranked sprinter; Oscar Moore Jr., Hall of Fame coach and Olympian, 5 - time NCAA Division III Coach of the Year; and Fred Samara, Princeton Head Coach, Olympic Decathlete, USA Olympic Coach, National Coach, and Hall of Famer. In addition, I was a physical education teacher for 10 years.

I am a coach who really focuses on mechanics. I believe to maximize your performance, one must cover ALL areas. I even count steps. Let’s face it. To get better, we are talking about seconds, or fractions of a second. If I am your coach, I will look at every possible way for you to improve. We will get fit, and we WILL perform!
