About Me

Teaching is about creating and providing a supportive environment in which a student can learn. My name is Brenda Lea, and I have been teaching in this manner for over 30 years in a private, tutorial environment. I have a passion for people and for empowering students to achieve their dreams and goals academically.As a mother of six children – I know how students think and learn. Not all students fit in the classroom model of “one size fits all” education: I have taught five-year olds to read at a 4 th grade level, and I have placed 5 th grade students into an algebra curriculum. My teaching strategy is to determine each individual student’s needs and strengths and to accelerate them to the highest level possible. There is no “box” for students; think outside of the box and achieve the highest goals possible.

I will be your facilitator and mentor to explore the world of education and take you to the heights you can achieve. My focus has been Teaching English as a Second Language (TESOL) since 2006, and I have advanced certification to teach TOEFL Prep courses. My students have been from 19 countries, and I have helped international athletes gain admission to Ivy League universities. Technology is changing the way we learn and what we learn as it opens up many opportunities for a variety of tutors with different approaches to learning. I look forward to coming alongside you as you enhance your English and achieve your dreams!
