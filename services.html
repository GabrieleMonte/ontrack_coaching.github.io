Our Educational Services

Subject
Tutoring

We offer tutoring in a variety of subjects, extending from STEM to Humanities courses. Get advice about how to best approach courses, engage with assignments, and prepare for exams. Learn new strategies, techniques, and skills to increase productivity and create a sustainable scheduling system for all day-to-day activities. Individual or group lessons are available for students of any age seeking to enhance their academic performance

ACT & SAT
Exam Prep

Here you can find extensive preparation for all parts of the SAT and ACT. Learn effective problem solving techniques and test taking strategies to help you manage your time on test day. Review challenging subject areas to ensure a more thorough understanding of test material. Individual or group lessons are available for high school students seeking to succeed the first time in these tests or improve upon past scores.

TOEFL & ESL
Services

Individual or group classes for all levels of English proficiency: beginner, intermediate, or advanced. Our ESL tutors are TESOL certified and can assist you to enhance your English skills in one of the four main skill areas: Reading & Listening/input or Speaking and Writing/output. We provide basic training on how to approach/tackle the test as well as specialized preparation in order to raise your score by a minimum of 10-20 points.

Real-Time Video Call Coaching & Analysis

Get real-time access to coaches of the highest level for practice sessions. Professional technical analysis and immediate feedback will help take your athletic performance to the next level. Our virtual coaching is especially useful for athletes who are geographically isolated from elite coaching, and for those who are simply looking for coaching at the highest level. The most easy, reliable, and time-flexible way to access world-class coaching.

Elite
Training Plan

Ready to start training at the highest level? Our monthly Elite Training Plans are created by our world-class coaches and are curated for each athlete’s specific needs and goals. With unparalleled athletic reputation, our coaches produce training plans designed to extract the best performance from athletes, while also ensuring their health using calculated training blocks and peak-timing.

Elite
Strength Development

To be able to build, first you must have a strong, reliable base. Our monthly Elite Strength Development plans, uniquely created for each athlete and body-type, will help athletes build and maintain strength and lower their risk of injury. From sprinting to cross country, all track and field athletes require a form of strength training to perform at the highest level, and our esteemed group of coaches are eager to share their knowledge to help you succeed.
