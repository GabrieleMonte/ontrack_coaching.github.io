About Me

My name is Robby Andrews, member of the 2016 United States Olympic Team. While coaching is my profession, running is my passion. I have been coaching youth runners for 10 years, specializing in middle to long distance events (800m-10,000m). From first time runners to national champions, my goal for every athlete I work with is to develop their interest in the sport and encourage long term development.

Middle to long distance running is as much an art as it is a science, and my creative approach to each athlete I coach is backed by scientific fundamentals that every runner should utilize.
