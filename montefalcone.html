About Me

Hey, this is Gab. I am a Physics graduate from Princeton University, where I also competed as a varsity athlete in the Track & Field team. At Princeton, I worked for three years as a Tutor and Learning Consultant for the McGraw Center for Teaching & Learning. In my time there, I worked with more than 30 students from a variety of disciplines, developing with each of them an individualized and strategic approach to learning that enables the student to make the most of lectures, precepts and readings; to manage their time and to achieve their goals while maintaining a healthy balance.

I look forward to work with students of any age and help them thrive in STEM courses and standardized tests through a lasting commitment to promoting success.
