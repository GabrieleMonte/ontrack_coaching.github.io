About Me

Hi, I’m coach P! I graduated from The College of New Jersey in 2010. I was a 4x Division III All American, hold 7 school records in various sprint and hurdle events, as well as a 7x conference champ. after TCNJ, I went on to complete a graduate assistantship at Rockford University in Illinois where I received my Masters in Education. During my time at Rockford, I truly enjoyed coaching and mentoring student-athletes. I love working one on one with student-athletes because I truly understand the commitment, dedication and hard work required to become successful. As a member of the organization Athletes in Action, I spent my summers working with young athletes from all over the country and the world. I lead coaching clinics for children of various ages, and spoke with different groups about my experiences on and off the track. I have a passion for teaching and coaching. Any role where I can provide a positive and meaningful educational experience is very rewarding to me. In my personal life, I am a married mama of two rambunctious and adventurous boys, who love to have fun! My family and I live in the beautiful mountains of Colorado where we ski, hike, bike, camp and enjoy all that nature has to offer.

I’m here to encourage you to believe in yourself and your goals. Together, we will cultivate success!
