Eric Plummer

About Me

Hi, my name is Eric Plummer and I’m a shot putter who graduated from Princeton University with a degree in Economics and earned a Master’s in Business Management for Wake Forest University (NC). In my athletic career, I have had the honor of competing on the world stage at the 2008 IAAF Junior World Championships in Bydgoszcz, Poland after winning the US Junior National title in shot put. At Princeton, I was a 4x NCAA qualifier, 5-time Ivy League Champion, winning three outdoor titles and two indoor titles in the shot put. I served as one of the team captains my senior year and led our squad to a team Ivy League title. From over 10 years of shot put (and throwing overall) at an elite level, I have the ability to share my expertise with throwers of all levels to rethink, refine and elevate their performance. Since my athletic career, I have transitioned into the business world with roles at Capital One, Lyft and several other businesses where I have been able to lead strategy, analytics and operations projects that drove significant value. I look forward to working with you and helping you to maximize your experience as a student-athlete while also preparing for life after athletics.
